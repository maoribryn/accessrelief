About Us

Delivering on a promise to address inconsistency

  1. AccessRelief provides tele-medicine services for people who seek alternative treatment options, based on holistic interventions to enhance overall health, improve comfort or alleviate specific issues that are currently not addressed 

    Our Story
    Over our Medical careers, we see that the same biases arise when it comes to access. Medicinal Cannabis was following this path too. People most likely to be harmed by MC were not having their requirements met, and so are left to fend for themselves with the Gangs. Using our knowledge of Primary Care funding in NZ, we can work with the system to offer people easier access, lower consultation fees (in some cases free), offering advice on usage optimisation in accordance with their symptoms, thereby reducing harm to our communities.
  1. Our Vision
    Equitably transform lives through advanced patient outcomes, incorporating holistic lifestyle interventions, plant-based medicines including medical cannabis.
  2. Our Mission
    Empowering people through education, support and guidance to improve their health.
