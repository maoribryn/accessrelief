About the Brief Consult
Our $30 consult is a short 3–5 minute telehealth or phone appointment.
It’s designed for patients who are likely to be eligible for a prescription and have no complex health concerns.
This brief session is usually sufficient to begin treatment with an initial short prescription.
It may require a subsequent consult to explore any medical issues in more detail.
